
# Fire Safety Monitoring System

## üåü Features  

### *For Users:*

- üîç *AI-Driven Pre-Assessment*  
   - Upload your fire safety plan to have it automatically analyzed by our AI.  
   - The AI provides a safety compliance score based on the submitted plan.  
   - If your safety score is above 60%, you can proceed to apply for your NOC.  

- üìù *NOC Application*  
   - Easily apply for your fire safety NOC directly through the platform.  
   - Submit all necessary documents, make payments, and track your application status.  

- üì± *Real-Time Notifications*  
   - Receive real-time SMS updates about your application‚Äôs progress.  
   - Stay informed with alerts on important events like approval or inspection updates.  

- üìä *Application Tracking*  
   - Track the status of your NOC application in real-time.  
   - Receive timely updates on any necessary follow-up actions.  

- üíª *User-Friendly Interface*  
   - Simplified dashboard for managing applications and monitoring safety compliance.  
   - Easy-to-use navigation for all users, whether you're applying or tracking.

### *For Inspectors:*

- üîë *Login with Credentials*  
   - Inspectors must log in using their credentials on the mobile app to get started with their daily tasks.  

- üìÖ *Task Assignment*  
   - Once logged in, inspectors can select their assigned tasks for the day.  

- ü§≥ *Face Verification*  
   - Inspectors are required to verify their identity using Face API (face-api.js) for security purposes.  

- üìç *Geolocation Verification*  
   - Inspectors must confirm their geolocation before starting the inspection, ensuring they are at the correct location.  

- üìù *Inspection Process*  
   - Inspectors can start the inspection process once all verifications are complete.  
   - They need to upload images related to the inspection and verify them before submitting the final report.  

### *For Admin:*

- üîë *Login with Credentials*  
   - Admins must log in through the web application to manage the platform.  

- üìë *Report Assessment*  
   - The admin reviews the submitted inspection reports and decides to either approve or reject the application.  

- üìç *Track Inspector Locations*  
   - Admins can view ongoing inspections and track inspector locations on an interactive map.  

- ‚ûï *Add Inspectors*  
   - Admins have the ability to add new inspectors to the system, ensuring the team stays up-to-date.  

- üìä *Analytics Dashboard*  
   - Admins can access an analytics dashboard to monitor the performance and status of inspections, NOCs, and applications.

## üéØ Use Cases  

*For Building Owners/Managers*  
- Assess the safety compliance of your building by uploading your fire safety plan.  
- Apply for an NOC directly through the platform if the AI score is above 60%.  
- Track your application status and stay updated with SMS notifications.

*For Inspectors*  
- Login, select your daily tasks, and proceed with inspections after completing security verifications.  
- Upload images, verify them, and submit detailed reports for approval.

*For Admins*  
- Review submitted inspection reports, approve or reject NOCs, and manage inspector assignments.  
- Monitor inspection status and view real-time locations of inspectors on the map.
